Privacy Policy for Poker Merge 2048

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Zhongwang Games ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Poker Merge 2048 mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Game Progress Data: Your scores, levels, coins, and unlocked avatars are stored locally on your device.
  • In-App Purchase Information: When you purchase the "Remove Ads" option, the transaction is processed by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not store your payment information.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Game sessions, features used, and gameplay statistics.
  • Advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob to serve personalized ads (you can opt-out in device settings).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining the App's functionality
  • Saving your game progress locally
  • Displaying advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • Processing in-app purchases
  • Improving game balance and user experience
  • Analyzing crash reports and technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Google AdMob (Advertising)

AdMob collects device identifiers to serve personalized ads. You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings.

4.2 Google Play Services / Apple App Store (In-App Purchases)

Payment processing is handled by Google/Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All game progress is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers.
  • No User Accounts: Our App does not require account registration or login.
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all local game data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is rated for general audiences and does not contain age-inappropriate content.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of personalized ads (in device settings)
  • Delete your game data (by uninstalling the App)
  • Request information about data collection (contact us below)

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
